MODULE: The Blackmoor Lighthouse

The fishing village of Blackmoor has lost three sailors this month. Their empty boats returned scratched and reeking of brine. The investigators begin at the Salt & Anchor Inn, where Martha the innkeeper watches the door and the harbor map hangs by the hearth.

The map hides a tide table with three dates circled. The old lighthouse on the northern cliff still burns each night, though Elias Crane is rarely seen.

Keeper notes: Elias drowned two years ago. A Deep One thrall now wears his face and uses the light to wreck boats. The lamp room hides human teeth inside the lens.
